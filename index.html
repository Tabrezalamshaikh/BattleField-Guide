üõ°Ô∏è Phase 2: Staying Alive and Maneuvering (The Circle)
1.Tracking the Safe Zone (White Circle): The map features a White Circle which defines the safe play area. This circle shrinks periodically, forcing players closer together. Your primary objective is to constantly move toward and stay inside this safe zone.
2.Avoiding the Blue Zone: The area outside the White Circle is the Blue Zone. Any player caught in the Blue Zone will take continuous damage. The damage intensifies as the game progresses, making the Blue Zone a hard boundary that guides players' movement.
3.Rotation and Travel: Since the map is large, players must find vehicles (like the Buggy, UAZ, or Dacia) to traverse distances quickly and stay ahead of the shrinking safe zone.

üí• Phase 3: Engagements and Strategy (The Mid-Game)
1.Combat (Engaging): As the safe zone shrinks, player density increases, leading to more frequent fights. You must use tactical cover, choose whether to snipe, flank, or rush enemies, and utilize different terrains to your advantage.
2.Airdrops: Planes occasionally drop special Care Packages that contain powerful, rare weapons (like the AWM sniper or Level 3 gear). Securing these is often high-risk but provides a significant advantage.
3.Positional Play: Strategic positioning becomes vital. Securing high ground or finding good natural cover (rocks, trees, buildings) is essential for surviving the mid-to-late game.

üèÜ Phase 4: The Final Showdown (Victory)
1.The Final Circle: The safe zone becomes extremely small, pushing the few remaining players into a confined area. This is the most intense, high-stakes part of the game.
2.Last Man/Team Standing: Players fight fiercely until all opposing players or teams are eliminated.
3.Chicken Dinner: The last surviving player or team wins the match and is rewarded with the iconic phrase: "WINNER WINNER CHICKEN DINNER!"
